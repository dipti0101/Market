Metal Brake Pads are friction components used in vehicle braking systems, made primarily from metallic compounds to provide high performance and durability, especially in high-temperature and heavy-duty applications.

The German Metal Brake Pad market is experiencing moderate growth, driven by the automotive industry's focus on safety and performance. In 2023, total production reached 45 million units, with passenger vehicles accounting for 65% of market value. The market saw a 10% increase in demand for copper-free metal brake pads in 2023, reflecting environmental regulations and sustainability trends. High-performance metal brake pads for sports and luxury vehicles are growing at 5% annually. The market is witnessing a trend towards noise reduction technologies, with a 25% year-over-year increase in R&D investments for quieter metal brake pad formulations. Electric vehicle segment demand for regenerative braking-compatible metal pads grew by 30% in 2023, adapting to the evolving automotive landscape.
