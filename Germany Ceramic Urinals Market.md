Ceramic Urinals are sanitary fixtures made from vitreous china or porcelain, designed for use in public restrooms, commercial buildings, and some residential settings, offering durability and ease of cleaning.

The German Ceramic Urinals market is growing moderately, driven by commercial construction activities, renovations in public facilities, and innovations in water-saving technologies. In 2023, total unit sales reached 280,000, with waterless urinals accounting for 25% of market value. The market saw a 15% increase in demand for smart, sensor-operated ceramic urinals in 2023, enhancing hygiene and water efficiency. Wall-hung urinals are growing at 4.5% annually, preferred for their space-saving design and ease of cleaning. The market is witnessing a trend towards antimicrobial glazes, with a 20% year-over-year increase in sales of urinals with built-in hygiene protection. Adoption of modular urinal systems for quick installation grew by 10% in 2023, particularly in renovation projects. The industry is focusing on developing ultra-low flush urinals, with a 30% increase in R&D investments for water-conserving technologies that maintain effective flushing performance.
