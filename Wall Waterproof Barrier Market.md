The wall waterproof barrier is a waterproof material installed on the surface of the building's exterior wall or outside the inner insulation layer to prevent moisture from penetrating and accumulating inside the wall, and to protect the building structure and the internal materials of the wall from moisture. Wall waterproof barriers are usually made of polymer materials, which can be film-like or coating-like, which can effectively prevent moisture from intruding into the building interior. The installation of the wall waterproof barrier should follow the corresponding installation requirements and building codes to ensure its effectiveness and reliability.
