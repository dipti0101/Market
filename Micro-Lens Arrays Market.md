Micro-Lens array is an array composed of lenses with micron aperture and relief depth. It not only has the basic functions of traditional lens such as focusing and imaging, but also has the characteristics of small unit size and high integration, which makes it able to complete the functions that traditional optical elements can’t accomplish, and can form many new optical systems.
