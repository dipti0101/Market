Latin America Butene Propylene Copolymer Market
The Latin America Butene Propylene Copolymer market size was valued at US$ 143 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 198 million by 2030, at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

Butene Propylene Copolymer is a thermoplastic polymer combining properties of both polypropylene and polybutene, used in various applications including packaging, automotive parts, and consumer goods.

The Latin American Butene Propylene Copolymer market is experiencing moderate growth, driven by demand in flexible packaging and automotive industries. Brazil, Mexico, and Colombia account for 75% of the regional market. In 2023, the packaging industry consumed 50% of Butene Propylene Copolymer in Latin America. The market saw a 14% increase in demand for high-clarity grades in 2023, particularly for food packaging applications. Use in automotive interior components is growing at 7% annually. Key manufacturers are focusing on developing grades with enhanced impact resistance, with an 18% year-over-year increase in product launches featuring improved toughness. The industry is also witnessing a trend towards recyclable formulations, with a 25% rise in R&D investments for circular economy-compatible copolymers.
