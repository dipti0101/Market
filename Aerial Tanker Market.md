Report Overview

Air tankers are aircraft that refuel aircraft and helicopters in flight. Mostly converted from large transport aircraft or strategic bombers. There are also shipborne aerial tankers and stealth tankers. Its role can increase the range of the tanker, extend the endurance time, increase the effective load, and improve the combat capability of the aviation. Most of the refueling equipment of the aerial refueling machine is installed at the rear of the fuselage, and a few are installed in the pod under the wing, which is controlled by the pilot or the fueler.
