Report Overview

Compressed air foam fire truck is also called air foam fire truck. This compressed air foam truck is loaded with 5 tons of water and 0.4 tons of high multiple foam. The vehicle has small water consumption, strong foam fire penetration, and minimal re-ignition. The features of high fire extinguishing efficiency and long range of foam have strong practicability in fighting high-rise buildings, oil and other fires.
