Indium gallium alloy is an emerging hot material at present. It is a eutectic alloy formed by melting gallium metal and metal at high temperature. It is a highly conductive liquid metal. Gallium-indium alloy is liquid at room temperature. The ratio of gallium indium alloy can be customized, and it is produced under the protection of nitrogen to prevent oxidation and pollution. Conventional gallium-indium alloy ratios are Ga:In=75.5:24.5 by weight, Ga:In=74.5:25.5 by weight, Ga:In=80:20 by weight, and so on.
