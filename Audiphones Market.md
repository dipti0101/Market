Audiphones are a small loudspeaker that amplifies the original inaudible sound, and then uses the residual hearing of the hearing impaired to send the sound to the auditory center of the brain and feel the sound. It is mainly composed of five parts: microphone, amplifier, earphone, power supply and volume control. Hearing aids are divided into air conduction hearing aids and bone conduction hearing aids according to the conduction mode; according to the usage mode, they are classified into box-type, glasses-type, hairpin-type, back-a-ear, in-ear, canal, and deep-canal audiphones
