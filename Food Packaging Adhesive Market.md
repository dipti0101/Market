China Food Packaging Adhesive Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2024-2030
The China Food Packaging Adhesive market size was valued at US$ 1.28 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 1.76 billion by 2030, at a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

Food Packaging Adhesives are specialized adhesives designed for use in food packaging applications, meeting strict safety and regulatory requirements to ensure they do not contaminate food products. These adhesives provide strong bonds for various packaging materials while being safe for indirect food contact.

China's Food Packaging Adhesive market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the expanding food processing industry and changing consumer preferences for convenience foods. In 2023, China consumed approximately 420,000 metric tons of food packaging adhesives, with 90% produced domestically. The flexible packaging sector remains the largest consumer, accounting for 60% of demand, followed by rigid packaging at 25% and labels at 15%. The market saw a significant boost from the rapid growth of e-commerce and food delivery services, which led to a 18% increase in demand for takeaway food packaging in 2023. Technological advancements have focused on developing adhesives with enhanced barrier properties, with new formulations offering 30% better moisture resistance compared to 2020 standards. The market is witnessing strong growth in water-based and hot melt adhesives, increasing at 7% and 9% annually respectively, driven by their food safety profile and efficiency in high-speed packaging lines. In 2023, new food safety regulations led to a 25% increase in adoption of FDA and EU-compliant adhesive systems. The industry faces challenges from fluctuating raw material prices, particularly for petrochemical-based ingredients, but innovations in bio-based adhesives are helping to mitigate this issue. Emerging applications in active and intelligent packaging are showing promise, with adhesives for oxygen scavengers and time-temperature indicators growing at 15% CAGR, albeit from a small base of 2% market share. Looking ahead, the market is expected to benefit from the growing trend of sustainable packaging, with compostable and recyclable adhesives projected to grow at 12% CAGR through 2030. The development of antimicrobial adhesives for food packaging is also gaining traction, with R&D investments in this area increasing by 40% in 2023 in response to heightened hygiene concerns post-pandemic.
