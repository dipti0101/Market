Electronic grade zirconium sulfate is soluble in water and sulfuric acid, slightly soluble in ethanol, insoluble in hydrocarbons, and releases a lot of heat when dissolved in water, and the aqueous solution is strongly acidic. It can be used as a tanning agent for white leather to make the leather surface delicate and elastic. It can also be used as a raw material for preparing zirconium compounds, and can be used as a lubricant, catalyst carrier, etc.
