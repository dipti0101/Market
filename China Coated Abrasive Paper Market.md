China Coated Abrasive Paper Market Global Outlook and Forecast 2024-2030
The China Coated Abrasive Paper market size was valued at US$ 985.3 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 1.32 billion by 2030, at a CAGR of 5.0% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

Coated Abrasive Paper consists of a paper backing coated with abrasive materials, used for sanding, polishing, and finishing in woodworking, metalworking, and various industrial applications.

China's Coated Abrasive Paper market is growing steadily, driven by the country's large manufacturing base and construction industry. In 2023, China produced approximately 1.8 billion square meters of coated abrasive paper, with 75% consumed domestically and 25% exported. The woodworking industry remains the largest consumer, accounting for 40% of demand, followed by metalworking at 30% and automotive refinishing at 20%. The market benefited from the recovery of the furniture manufacturing sector, which led to a 15% increase in demand for fine-grit abrasive papers in 2023. Technological advancements have focused on developing longer-lasting and more efficient abrasives, with new products offering 30% longer lifespan compared to 2020 standards. The market is seeing strong growth in waterproof abrasive papers, increasing at 8% annually, driven by demands in automotive and marine applications. In 2023, stricter workplace safety regulations led to a 25% increase in the use of dust-free sanding systems, boosting demand for compatible abrasive papers. The industry faces challenges from competition with non-woven abrasives and digital sanding technologies, but innovations in multi-functional abrasive papers are helping maintain market share. Emerging applications in 3D printing post-processing are showing promise, with specialized abrasive papers for additive manufacturing growing at 20% CAGR, albeit from a small base of 2% market share. Looking ahead, the market is expected to benefit from China's ongoing urbanization and infrastructure development, with abrasive papers for construction and renovation projected to grow at 6% CAGR through 2030.
