Clinical chemistry analyzers are used for the analysis of samples such as blood, serum, and urine for the detection of analytes. These analyzers are used to detect and measure protein, enzyme, and electrolyte levels present in the sample. The measurement is based on the techniques such as photometric and colorimetric testing, potentiometry, and agglutination.Consequently, the resultant high sample volume requiring high capability analyzing devices is driving the market growth.
