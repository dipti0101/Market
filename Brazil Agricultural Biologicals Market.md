Brazil Agricultural Biologicals Market
The Brazil Agricultural Biologicals market size was valued at US$ 456 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 687 million by 2030, at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

Agricultural biologicals are naturally derived products used in crop protection, enhancement, and nutrition, including biopesticides, biostimulants, and biofertilizers.

Brazil's agricultural biologicals market is experiencing rapid growth, driven by increasing adoption of sustainable farming practices and the push for reduced chemical inputs. In 2023, biopesticides accounted for 45% of the agricultural biologicals market in Brazil, followed by biostimulants at 30% and biofertilizers at 25%. The market saw an 18% increase in demand for microbial-based products in 2023, particularly for soil health improvement and pest control. Use of pheromone-based pest management solutions is growing at 12% annually. Key players are investing heavily in R&D, with a 22% year-over-year increase in spending on novel biological formulations and delivery systems. The industry is also witnessing a trend towards integrated biological-chemical crop protection programs, with a 25% rise in adoption of bio-based products as part of resistance management strategies. Brazil's large organic farming sector and the need to meet stringent export market requirements continue to drive agricultural biologicals demand, with a 15% increase in biological product use among conventional farmers transitioning to more sustainable practices.
