EUV Dust-proof Film is a key component of the Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography process, which refers to a thin film that is spread over the photomask and on which the machine draws the circuit to be embossed on the wafer, preventing the photomask from being contaminated by airborne dust or volatile gases and reducing damage to the photomask.
